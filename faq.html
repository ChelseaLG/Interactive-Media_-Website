    FAQ -

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I change the Saving Location?
    Go into the directory of Pico8 and then move the Folder to your desired Saving Location. Restart the Pico8 client and load your progress. If you still have difficulties after that, then please use our Support-Form
    What do I do when I get an Error?
    If you can not read the Error, then please use our Support-Form
    How do I load my Game?
    Start Pico8 and then type: Load("The Name of your Game"). You should the get directed to the main Menu. But you can also Load your last Save directly from Pico8. For that please type: Load("Last Safe").
